I am Civil/Environmental Engineering Major at UMD. My goals beyond college like in driving the green energy initiative --born from my interests in the environment, human development, and energy systems. I envision a greener world in the future where man and nature are in some form of harmony, and I am trying to do my part in making that a reality.

On a more personal level, I enjoy the comfort of my own room when I can because life gets very busy for me. In other words, my free is sacred because this is when I get to read a lot of webtoons and comics and delve into a world of fantasy. Besides that, the time I get with my friends is also invaluable to me because the time I spent with them comes with joy and hope for the future.

It is important for me to note that my future aspirations are not absolute. I am constantly changing and refining and so are my beliefs and interests. who knows where I will be in 10 years. I may have an idea of where I would like to be, but I remain open to the tides of change and possibility
